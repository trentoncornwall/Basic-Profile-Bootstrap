About Me

Hello, small town Iowa raised. Which is very different from where I am now, Denver. I grew up a twin brother and many horses. Family and I would often take weekend vacations to a ranch were we road around on hourses, played in the woods. I eventually started bull riding, which lasted about 2 years.


After graduating high-school in my small town, I went to a few smaller colleges before landing at the Univeristy of Iowa. Where I majored in Informatics. Upon graduating, I really wasn't interested in doing much of what I learned. But one class, did stand out to me. Which was a Computer Human Interactions. How people interacted with computers. We built a webpage, using BootStrap, MySQL, PHP. This was my absolute favorite class. I knew I wanted to do that for a living.


So I started teaching myself as much as I could about web-development. I wanted to accerate the learning, so I invested in myself once again and started my journery and a Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp at the University of Denver. Where I'm excited to learn MongoDB and Node.JS. Which is what OpenTable, the company I work for uses.
